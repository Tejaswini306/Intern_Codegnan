Awards and Achievements

Bunny's performance in the films such as Arya (2004), Desamuduru (2007), Parugu (2008), Vedam (2010), Race Gurram (2014), Rudhramadevi (2015) and Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo (2020) fetched him various accolades including six CineMAA Awards, six Filmfare South Awards, three Nandi Awards and three SIIMA awards.

In 2004 he recieved best male debut award by CineMAA for his performance in the film Gangotri.He also recieved CineMAA best actor awards for the films Arya in 2005, Desamuduru in 2008, Parugu in 2009, Racegurram in 2015 and his phenomenal performance in Rudramadevi in the year 2017

Allu Arjun recieved three nandi awards as best actor for the films Arya and Vedam and as best character Actor for critically acclaimed film Rudhramadevi.

The movie ‘Pushpa’ made the stylish star of Tollywood an icon star.Allu Arjun got craze not only at pan India level but also at international level with this movie. Meanwhile, Allu Arjun received the ‘GQ Leading Man of the Year’ award for 2022 at the ‘Gentlemen’s Quarterly’ Awards organized by a magazine in Central London.Usually this award is mostly given to Bollywood stars. Deepika, Ranveer, Vicky Kaushal and Karthik Aryan have received this award in the past. But Allu Arjun is the first actor to receive this award from Tollywood.
